Divine Shatter

Your deepest fears and desires consume you

What began as a harmless video game quickly became a trap as players of the virtual dive realm Divine Shatter are suddenly stuck and cannot return to the real world. The game is only accessible to those who were mentored by another player. Faced with the reality that they are forcibly locked into this world, the party is hired by the Black Queen, whose goal is to find a way to end the game. The party traverses many different kingdoms, navigating political intrigue, a digital bug that spreads like a disease, and rumors that the game's developers are not what they seem.



Meet the Characters

Cyrus

  • Gnome
  • Wizard
  • 12 Years Old

Cyrus is a fun-loving pacifist that enjoys spending time with the party. Though he typically doesn't like to resort to violence, he will use force when he feels he has no other choice.

Rudy

  • High Elf
  • Rogue
  • 12 Years Old

Rudy is obssessed with the color pink and enjoys lots of sweets. She also attempts to make up for her youth by acting tough, but truly she just wants to fit in with the people around her.

Leo

  • Wood Elf
  • Druid
  • 21 Years Old

Leo is a semi-serious character with a greedy knack for money and finding ways to get out of doing something.

Mitchie

  • Human

  • ???

Mitchie is of unknown age and has multiple personalities.

Lith

  • Earth Genasi
  • Monk
  • 17 Years Old

While Lith is young, she does not act like it. She tends to be on the quiet side, but is very go-with-the-flow when it comes to the ridiculous antics of the rest of the party.

Markus

  • Half-Elf
  • Warlock
  • 10 - 1200 Years Old

Markus is a very distrustful man who can come off a bit rude quite often. This stems from the need to keep his secret that he is, in fact, an NPC that was given sentience instead of a player like the rest of the party.
