Meet the Characters

Garos

  • Halfling
  • Barbarian / Warlock
  • 26 Years Old

Garos is a roudy healer of a halfling tribe in the northern tundra. He has an herbal tea addiction. He is seeking knowledge of an apothecary while struggling with his temper in hopes to become a better healer for his tribe.

Dom Dom

  • Half Orc
  • Barbarian
  • Late 20's

Dominic was born and raised in a thieves guild and dreamed to be the best thief in the guild. However, in practice, he fell short, crashing into things, having loud footsteps, and other stealthy shortcomings. The guild kicked him out, and with his parents having disowned him, Dominic was on his own. He seeks to prove the guild wrong and make an infamous name for himself.

Kieran

  • Half Elf
  • Paladin
  • 30 Years Old

Kieran is a very kind and loyal friend to most, stemming from his good natured characteristics that were taught to him by his mentor.

Limba

  • Dragonborn
  • Fighter/Warlock
  • 20 Years Old

An adventerous child, Limba wandered off from her family and learned to survive on her own. She discovered a love for magic and made a pact with demon Bobberis to pursue magical powers. Limba is childlike and gullibe, but weilds a confident spirit which makes it hard to upset her.

Julien

  • Satyr
  • Bard
  • 55 Years Old

Julien is very talkative and always up for adventure. She loves spreading and gaining any musical knowledge she can get. She may be a bit loud or confused at times, but she can lie herself out of any situation. Her favorite colors are forest green and orange and she has a twin brother named Julius.
