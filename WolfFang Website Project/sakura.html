Sakura Syndicate

Only the Gods know now

In the world of Ecrea, year 3030, the capital, Favor City, was once renowned for its trade and economic power, however it is now drowned in crime and twisted plots to get to the top. The head of Syndicate Pharmaceuticals, Jay Syndicate, is suspected of poisoning his Cyber enhancements. His daughter Sarah is the head of the Underground, an organization for those who don't have anywhere else to go. Most people in the Underground seek revenge for the pain Jay has caused them. Our adventurers navigate the crime-ridden city and try their best to get to the bottom of Jay Syndicate's schemes.



Meet the Characters

Permaris

  • Wood Elf
  • Warlock


Permaris is a criminal that kills for his patron Beelzebub. He stays quiet, careful not to give away too much information about himself, although he is very forgetful. His mother was killed in front of him at a young age.

Akira

  • Human
  • Monk


Akira believes in doing the job the boss tells him to do. He is trained in martial arts and does the jobs to help his mom pay the rent.

Paxton

  • Arakocra
  • Monk


Due to a genetic experiment, Paxton, once a human, was turned into a parrot. He tries to survive on the streets by taking odd jobs while wishing to relieve his glory days.

Tohjan

  • Dragonborn
  • Barbarian / Rogue


Tohjan has always been within the walls of one of the highest corporations. Put to service as corporate security at the age of 7, he moved up the ranks alongside his father. When his father defended Tohjan in front of the corporate leaders, his father was killed in his place and Tohjan moved to his father's rank. Eventually the company collapsed, leaving him unemployed and he became a bouncer to make money.
