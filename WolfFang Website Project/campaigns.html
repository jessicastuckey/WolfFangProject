Campaigns

Delve into the worlds of our ongoing campaigns

Wolf Fang D&D's campaigns are year-long and begin in the fall semester of the school year. The number of campaigns being run varies based on our available Dungeon Masters. Once campaigns are filled up and have begun, members who are not a part of campaigns are still free to engage socially in the club and take part in One-Shots. Find more information about upcoming One-Shots on our Events page.
